Sponsor Insight Chronicles

Insight Chronicles reaches readers interested in governance, digital public infrastructure, geopolitics, and technology policy — a focused, high-intent audience rather than a mass one. We're early and growing, which means sponsor placements right now come with founder-level attention and flexible formats. Rates below are a starting point; happy to discuss what fits your goals.

Ways to work with us

Newsletter sponsorship

A dedicated mention in the Insight Chronicles newsletter (single placement, clearly labeled "Sponsored"). Good for products/services relevant to governance, policy, dev tools, or research audiences.

Sponsored article

A long-form piece we write (or co-write with your input) on a topic relevant to our beat, clearly disclosed as sponsored content per our disclosure policy. We retain editorial control over framing and accuracy — no unverifiable claims.

Display placement

A banner/box placement on the homepage or article sidebar, billed monthly.

Ground rules

  • All sponsored content is clearly labeled — we don't run undisclosed native ads.
  • We don't accept sponsors in categories we cover critically.
  • Editorial independence: sponsors don't get to review or approve unrelated articles.
